Welcome to Barasa's Groceries

We are a family-owned grocery shop that has been serving our community for over 20 years. Our mission is to provide the freshest and highest quality products to our customers at affordable prices.
We take pride in offering a wide range of groceries, including fresh fruits, vegetables, dairy, cereals, and condiments.

Our dedicated team is committed to ensuring your shopping experience is enjoyable, and we look forward to serving you for many more years to come.
